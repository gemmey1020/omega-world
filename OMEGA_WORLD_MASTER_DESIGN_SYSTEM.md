STATUS: FOUNDER_APPROVED | CONCEPT: HYBRID (COMPANION + CURATOR)

1. Visual Philosophy: The Trusted Assistant

Core Goal: Combine the warmth and accessibility of a local neighborhood friend (Companion) with the elite, noise-free clarity of a high-end service (Curator).
2. Layout & Spacing (The Breathing Room)

    Background: Warm Off-White (#F8FAFC) for the global frame to reduce eye strain for elderly users.

    Margins: 24px global horizontal padding (The Curator influence) to provide a premium feel and prevent accidental taps.

    Grid: Strict single-column list on mobile. On Desktop, a centered 540px container to maintain the "Handheld" focus.

3. Shapes & Borders (Tactile Security)

    Border Radii: 20px for all vendor and product cards. This strikes the balance between the "Bubbly" 24px and the "Hard" 8px.

    Borders: 1.5px solid Slate-Grey (#E2E8F0). High visibility of card boundaries is crucial for users with declining vision.

    Shadows: Very soft, diffused shadows (0 10px 15px -3px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)) to create a "Physical Object" feel.

4. Typography & Accessibility (The Mom-Approved Standard)

    Fonts: - Prices & Scarcity: Bold Monospaced Navy (The Command influence) for absolute data clarity.

        Headings: Bold Deep Navy (#0F172A) - Minimum 20px for readability.

    Tap Targets: Minimum 56px x 56px for all interactive elements (larger than the standard 48px) to accommodate "Fat-Finger" errors or shaky hands.

5. The "Orange Laser" Logic (Actionable Dopamine)

    Primary CTA: Solid Orange (#F97316) for "Checkout" and "Join" buttons.

    Secondary Actions: White background with 2px Orange border for "Add to Cart" (+) buttons.

    Constraint: NO orange is used for decoration. It is a functional signal for "Success/Progress".

6. Vendor Status (Cognitive Clarity)

    Inactive/Closed: 100% Grayscale + 50% Opacity.

    Overlay: A subtle Deep Navy tint with a clear "Closed for Now" label in the center.

    Active: High saturation + Emerald Green pulsing dot next to "Checkout Ready".
