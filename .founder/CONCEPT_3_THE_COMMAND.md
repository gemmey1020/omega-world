# CONCEPT 3: THE COMMAND

## Philosophy

**Systemic Efficiency.** The psychological goal is absolute operational speed and data density. Designed for power users or intense localized buyers who know exactly what they want. It is utilitarian, highly organized, and leverages Deep Navy authority to the maximum.

## Layout Architecture

- **Pacing:** High-density, utilitarian grid. 8px vertical margins between tight items; 16px horizontal container padding.
- **Desktop vs. Mobile:** Mobile relies on dense 2-column or list-compact views for rapid scanning. Desktop expands into a multi-pane interface (e.g., Categories left, Items center, Cart locked right).

## Shapes & Borders

- **Border Radii:** Hybrid-technical. 4px radii. Clinical, exact, uncompromising.
- **Borders & Shadows:** Minimal shadows. Structure is dictated by hard 1px Deep Navy borders and solid background contrasts (`#F1F5F9`).
- **Inactive Vendors:** 100% Grayscale, 60% opacity, crushed into a compact "Archived/Closed" accordion at the bottom of the feed to immediately purge them from the primary cognitive viewing area.

## Navigation Flow

- **Back Button:** Text-based "<- Back" embedded directly into a compact sticky header.
- **Filters:** Embedded into a collapsing accordion or sticky sub-header to maximize vertical screen real estate for the actual products.
- **Search:** Command-K style dominance. The search bar is always visible and takes up 80% of the header width.

## The "Action Laser" (Orange Application)

- **Checkout/Add to Cart:** Orange is a literal laser—thin 2px active indicator lines on selected tabs, and dense, small rigid square buttons (`[+]`) for hyper-rapid cart addition. The checkout CTA is docked permanently to the bottom right.
- **Scarcity:** A strict, numeric progress bar. Deep Navy track with an Orange fill that visually depletes as seats/items are claimed, mapping urgency to physical geometry.

## PWA Feel

- **Interactions:** Utilitarian speed. Animation durations are slashed to `0ms - 100ms` max. Feedback is instant, color-based (flash of Orange on tap), bypassing physics for raw responsiveness.
